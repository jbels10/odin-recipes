Lemonade

A classic summer drink

Nothing is more refreshing on a hot summer day than a nice, ice cold glass of lemonade.

You only need three ingerdients to make this classic lemonade recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water, divided
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup fresh-squeezed lemon juice (seeds removed)

Instructions

  1. Combine 1 cup water and 1 cup sugar in a small saucepan. Place over medium-low heat and stir until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and set aside, allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  2. Pour 1 cup fresh-squeezed lemon juice into a pitcher. Add sugar/water mixture (simple syrup).
  3. Add remaining 5 cups (1180ml) water and stir well. Pour over ice and serve, or transfer to refrigerator and allow to chill several hours before serving.
